MUNICIPAL COORPORATION

OF INDIA

As per
the registration of Births and Deaths Act, 1969 in respect of births and deaths in a hospital, health center, maternity or nursing home or other like institutions, responsibility of informing the events to the registrar

On this website all the institutions have to register themselves.After successful registration the institution have to register the event occur (either birth or death) so that a unique serial no. get generated by the website which will further used as a verification by Municipal Office.

In this project we launch web app that will provide data to municipal office regarding birth or death of a person through the parent/guardian/relative.The Municipal Office will further generate the birth/death certificate in a certain duration after the validation of the documents submitted by the user.It will also help the government in census as this app will provide a database of birth and death in the country. As the app will also verify whether the event occur or not, so the personal inspection system will also get eliminated.
